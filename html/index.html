Dota Helper

Your best assistant in mastering the game Dota 2.

About

Our program solves the main problem for new users of the game Dota 2. As soon as players first encounter the game, it is difficult to choose heroes for the match, as there are a huge number of unique heroes in the game.

DotaHelper should allow users to improve their chances of winning, especially for beginners. Hero selection is the most important aspect in a match. Based on the data received from the match, our program will offer him the best hero.
